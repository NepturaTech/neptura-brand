# Brand Guidelines MVP
## Reglas de Ejecución Rápida

These are the non-negotiable rules for anyone producing Neptura brand materials.

---

### Logo

- Never alter the proportion of the "N" icon.
- Always use the white variant on dark backgrounds or photos.
- Never place the logo on patterned backgrounds without sufficient contrast.
- Minimum clear space: equal to the height of the "N" icon on all sides.

### Color

- **Signal Green** (`#10B981`) is used ONLY to highlight success states or key data points. Never use it as a full section background — it causes visual fatigue.
- **Dark BG** (`#0B0E14`) is the dominant surface. Ensure all text meets WCAG AA contrast (4.5:1 minimum).
- Never use pure black (`#000000`) as a background.

### Typography

- **Monospace** (JetBrains Mono) is reserved for numbers, coordinates, hashes, and financial values only. Do not use for paragraphs or body copy.
- **Inter** is the default for all UI text, headings, and body copy.

### Photography & Imagery

- Show technology, process, and dignified human work.
- Prohibido: photos of rural poverty, generic stock "nature", or nostalgic filters.
- Satellite imagery with UI overlays is always preferred over plain landscape photos.

### Language

| Use | Avoid |
|---|---|
| Intervención | Ayuda |
| Productor | Campesino |
| Validación | Revisión |
| Certeza Climática | Impacto ambiental (vague) |
| Plataforma de inteligencia | Consultora |
