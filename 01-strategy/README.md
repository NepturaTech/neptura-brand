## Key Decisions

- **Positioning pivot:** From "environmental consultancy" → "Climate Validation Infrastructure (SaaS)"
- **Core claim:** "The Climate Certainty Engine"
- **Sacrifice:** Emotional/humanitarian language in favor of financial and technical authority
- **Target:** Municipalities (B2G) + Coffee exporters (B2B) → Carbon market operators (B2B2B)
