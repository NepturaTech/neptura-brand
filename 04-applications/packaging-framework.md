# Packaging Framework
## Digital & Physical Touchpoints

---

### 1. The "Smart Tag" — Etiqueta Física (Saco de Café)

**Context:** Export coffee bag label.
**Dimensions:** 10 × 15 cm adhesive label.

**Visual Hierarchy:**
1. QR Code — large, center/right (links to immutable blockchain record)
2. Logo Neptura Science — small, top
3. Key data block:
   - `Carbon Footprint: -X kg CO₂`
   - `Origin: Cauca, CO`
   - `EUDR: Compliant ✓`
4. Blockchain Hash — small monospace text at bottom (e.g., `0x4a3f...c91b`)

**Design Notes:**
- Dark background with Signal Green accents.
- QR code must be printable at minimum 300 DPI.
- Use Blockchain Seal logo variant (circular) if space allows.

---

### 2. The "Certificate Asset" — PDF / NFT

**Concept:** Modernized financial bond or diploma — authority through design.

**Design Direction:**
- Guilloché border (security pattern), digitally modernized.
- Dark background or white, depending on print context.

**Required Content:**
- Rendered map of the certified land parcel.
- GPS coordinates of the parcel.
- Cryptographic signature (hash).
- Municipal or certification authority signature.
- Issue date, expiry, and standard (e.g., Gold Standard, EUDR).
- Neptura Science logo + "NEPTURA VERIFIED" seal.

**File Formats:** PDF (print), SVG/PNG (digital), optionally minted as NFT.
